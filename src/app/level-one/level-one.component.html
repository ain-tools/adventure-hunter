Quick Tutorial:

  • To open character skills sheet : Click on one of your allies in the board.
  • To move : Open your character skills sheet, click on Move and select your location on one of the highlighted boxes on the board.
  • To cast spells : Open your character skills sheet, click on Cast Spell and select your target. Energy is used.
  • To win : SLAUGHTER THEM ALL!!
  • End turn : Don't forget to end your turn after you're done!
  • Energy System : Used to cast spells. You start with 10,each turn regenerates your energy + 2.5 energy. Energy is capped off to 25.
